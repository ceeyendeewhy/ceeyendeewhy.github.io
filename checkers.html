Checkers61B

A classic game of checkers with a twist.

View more projects

All of the classic rules of checkers apply but with the introduction of two new pieces- the bomb and shield pieces.

Players alternate turns, only forward-diagonal movements are allowed with the exception of king pieces who may also move in backwards-diagonal directions.

Bomb pieces destroy themselves upon capturing an opponent's piece as well as all pieces within its radius except shield pieces.
