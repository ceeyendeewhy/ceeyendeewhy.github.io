Outside Snacks

A crowdsourced MEAN stack web app built at Outside Hacks 2015

Outside Snacks allows festival-goers to learn more about food vendors at the event.

Rate the food vendor out of 5 stars, mark your favorite item, and write a review.

The app is quick and simple to use.

A full list of food vendors at the festival is displayed on the home page.

You can toggle the "POPULAR" button to view the vendors with the least and most visitors.

Or sort vendors alphabetically or by rating.

Rate existing vendors as well to warn others of what to expect.
