Ngordnet

Investigating the history of English words and their relationships using Google's Ngram dataset and Princeton's Digraph class.

View more projects

The program allows you to find the ammount of times a word appeared in a particular year...

...and find hyponyms of words. (Hyponym: a descendant of a word. E.g. a "Maltese cat" is-a type of "cat".)

Apparently Menticirrhhus Americanus is a type of fish.

Additionally you can plot the popularity of words over time. It looks like the most important meal of the day is not as popular as lunch or dinner...
